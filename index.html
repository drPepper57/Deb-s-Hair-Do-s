Balayage / Highlights

Balayage (taken from the French word for 'sweeping') is a subtle freehand colouring technique. Colour is painted directly onto designated sections of the hair without using foil. The result is a more natural, sun-kissed hue, without any harsh contrast between colors.

Price: starting at £90
Keratin treatment and
Brazilian blow dry

A keratin treatment and Brazilian blow dry both work to smooth and de-frizz hair. While keratin treatments strengthen hair by replenishing lost keratin, Brazilian blow dry focuses on reducing curl and frizz while maintaining volume. Both leave hair shinier, more manageable, and humidity-resistant for several months.

Price: starting at £100
